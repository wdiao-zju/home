字符串的长度

  • 单对象
str_length("dasdada")
## [1] 7
  • 多对象
str_length(c("a", "dasda", ""))
## [1] 1 5 0

字符串取子集

a <- "dsadas"
str_sub(a, 1, 3)
## [1] "dsa"

字符串匹配

x <- c("apple", "bna", "pear")
str_view(x, pattern = "a")
str_view(x, "a$")
str_view(x, "^a")
